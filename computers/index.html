Computer Wizard Assembly Overview

Hello everyone and welcome to an indepth guide on how you can build your own computer! I love computers and I am sure you do too! We all use computers one way or another in our daily lives. Throughout this website, you will learn the basics of how to assemble a brand new computer from parts you have gathered. You will also learn about the basics of what those components do with one another and how each one helps to create computers that everyone uses today. There are many parts that go into assembling a computer and they each have their own role in allowing the computer to operate.

I am here to help you all learn about computers but also how to assemble one yourself! I have built over 10 computers and am here to help you build one yourself!
